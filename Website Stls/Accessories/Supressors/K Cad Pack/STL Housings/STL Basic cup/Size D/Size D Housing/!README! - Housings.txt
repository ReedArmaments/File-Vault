These are suppressor baffle housings, also known as the can, or simply the suppressor housing. A suppressor baffle housing is the main 
part of the suppressor, that holds the suppressor baffles. Pick the suppressor housing that has a caliber, that is at least 0.6 inches 
larger(recommended) than the largest caliber bullet you plan to shoot through it. Then pick the housings length, in accordence to the 
number of baffles you plan for it to hold.
